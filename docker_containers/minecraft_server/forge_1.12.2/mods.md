### Hardcore Darkness
    You have probably noticed it yourself but its never actually pitch black in minecraft. 
    Even if you are in a cave with no torches you will still be able to see something independent of your gamma setting. 
    This mod changes this, it removes the minimum block & sky light so if there is no light source its actually dark.
    - [curseforge](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/hardcore-darkness)
